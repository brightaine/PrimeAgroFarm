About Prime Agro Farm

Redefining modern dairy and agribusiness in Zirobwe, Luweero

Our Story

Prime Agro Farms Uganda Ltd was established in 2008 by Sebastian Rutah Ngambwa as a family business rooted in a vision: to redefine modern dairy and agribusiness in Zirobwe, Luweero. Starting from just 25 acres and nine cross-breed dairy cows under its earlier name Zirobwe Demonstration Farm (ZIDEFA), the farm has since expanded beyond 250 acres, of which about 100 acres are devoted to silage production ‚Äî supplying both the farm itself and neighbouring farmers.

Using Friesian and Jersey breeds managed under a semi-intensive system (silage, supplement feeds, and grazing), Prime Agro Farm boasts over 250 head of cattle, built modern infrastructure (calf shelters, resting zones, strong water systems) and invests in quality, animal welfare, and value-addition.

The farm has also developed a reputation for leadership in agriculture: it was the venue for the 2025 launch of the 11th Best Farmers Competition under the theme "Value Addition with Focus on Large Scale Farmers and Cooperatives," and in 2023 won high honours in that same competition. Additionally, Mr. Ngambwa is committed to mentorship and capacity building ‚Äî training other farmers in feed production, nourishment, and sustainable practices.

With an investment of over UGX 200 million in the early phase, Prime Agro Farm continues to demonstrate how combining innovation, scale, and a community-oriented approach can transform farming from subsistence to prosperity for the region and beyond.
